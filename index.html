⛓️ Disc Golf Tournament Heatmap

Visualize tournament schedules with a GitHub-style contribution graph

How to use:

  1. Go to discgolfscene.com/tournaments
  2. Search for tournaments (select your region, date range, etc.)
  3. Copy the entire page content (Ctrl+A, then Ctrl+C)
  4. Paste the HTML content in the textarea below
  5. Click "Generate Heatmap" to visualize the tournament schedule
